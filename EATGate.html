Enemy At The Gate


Enemy at the Gates is a gripping war drama set during the intense Battle of Stalingrad in World War II. The film follows a skilled Soviet sniper whose deadly accuracy turns him into a symbol of hope for his nation, leading to a high-stakes cat-and-mouse game with a top German marksman. The story blends action, suspense, and emotion against a dramatic wartime backdrop.

Main Actors:
Jude Law, Ed Harris, Rachel Weisz, Joseph Fiennes, Bob Hoskins

Genre: War, Drama, Thriller, Historical

Runtime: 2 hours 11 minutes
