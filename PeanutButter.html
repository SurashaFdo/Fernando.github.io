The Peanut Butter Falcon


The Peanut Butter Falcon is a heartfelt adventure about an unlikely friendship between a young man with Down syndrome who dreams of becoming a professional wrestler and a troubled drifter he meets along the way. Filled with warmth, humor, and optimism, the film explores freedom, acceptance, and finding your own path in life.

Main Actors:
Shia LaBeouf, Zack Gottsagen, Dakota Johnson

Genre: Adventure, Comedy, Drama, Coming-of-Age
